"Ensuring every animal has the right to a safe and happy life"

PAWS pet shelter was established in 1992 and has been caring for animals in Southland ever since. We promote the welfare of animals and provide care and shelter for abandoned pets. PAWS pet shelter has a variety of workers and volunteers who strive to improve the well being of all animals we get to see. A non profit organisation who would not be functioning without publics support, PAWS relys on the income from donations and the selling of our animals.
