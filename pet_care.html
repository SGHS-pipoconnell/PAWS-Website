Pet Care

The 5 Welfare Needs

Environment - A suitable place to live

Diet - The right food in the right amounts

Behaviour - Being able to behave normally

Company- For animals that need to live together

Health - Protecting your pet from pain, injury and disease

As a pet owner, what can I do?

● Assess your own pet’s quality of life by using the five welfare needs like a tick-list.

● If you think that you aren’t achieving one or more of the five welfare needs, change the way you keep or look after your pet so that you can achieve them. Ask your vet for advice if necessary.

● If you’re not sure whether you’re achieving the five welfare needs, or you’re not sure how to make changes for the better, ask your veterinary practice for help.
